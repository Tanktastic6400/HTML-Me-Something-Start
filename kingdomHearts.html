Kingdom Hearts 2
May Your Heart Be Your Guiding Key.

Sequel to Kingdom Hearts, a game produced and made by square enix in conjunction with Disney. You play as a young boy named Sora, who through no fault of his own leaves his home of Destiny Islands and goes on an adventure with his new friends Donald and Goofy, through various Disney worlds, in search of his old friends Rikku and Kairi from Destiny Islands.

Along the way you fly through space on a Gummi Ship (trademark pending), team up with Disney stars like Mulan, Captain Jack Sparrow from Pirates of the Caribbean, and even Jack Skellington from Nightmare Before Christmas. You fight evil, save some people and discover the power of friendship.

After playing the original Kingdom Hearts on a whim back when it first released, finding out that it had a sequel absolutely blew my little 11 year old mind. I remember the hours I would spend on Kingdom Hearts One. Now as an adult, without a single doubt in my mind, KH2 is forever a staple as one of my favorite all time games. Bigger maps, more convoluted and confusing storyline, more combat mechanics. I remember freaking out the first time combat forms were introduced into the series, I would spend hours trying to figure out how the Rage form worked. I only wish that I could experience it for the first time again. Oh, let's not forget about the reaction commands. It basically took you from the normal combat into an action scene/ sequence that was so cool compared to normal combat, I only ever replayed the game for those scenes alone.
